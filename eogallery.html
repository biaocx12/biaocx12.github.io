    Le Sacre

    The Coronation of Napoleon (French: Le Sacre de Napoléon) is a painting
    completed in 1807 by Jacques-Louis David, the official painter of Napoleon. The painting has imposing dimensions,
    as it is almost ten metres wide by approximately six metres tall.
    The crowning and the coronation took place at Notre-Dame de Paris,
    a way for Napoleon to make it clear that he was a son of the Revolution.
